Atlas

Zeta Atlas offers maps and a GIS platform to develop an implementation. Atlas covers flexible GIS functions such as creating custom areas (circle/radius, rectangular), measuring the distance of two different points, and analyzing contextual fraud. Fraud detection covers anomaly movements of tracked vessels (sailing speed, stopping), suspected illegal contacts, and more.

CC

Zeta CC (ClosedCaption) offers a machine learning text/number extraction process on secure and scalable Azure cloud and cognitive services. This method dramatically differs from traditional OCR methods that convert each character into digital ones. CC employs pre-trained machine learning models to identify numbers and text in various formats and tables, often found in working reports.
